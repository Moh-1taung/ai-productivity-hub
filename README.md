A workspace that maximises productivity and saves the time spent doing work tasks.
The application includes the following features:
SMART EMAIL GENERATOR (Creates emails for users)
MEETING NOTES SUMMARIZER (extracts important details from long notes)
AI TASK PLANNER (enables users to prioritise their tasks and also know when they need to be accomplished)
AI RESEARCH ASSISTANT (with just the input of a topic, the bot conducts the research and gives out important insights)
CHATBOT (allows for an immediate interaction between users and the bot. The conversation can be both professional or casual)
